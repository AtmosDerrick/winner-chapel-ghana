How do I join WFS

To join a WSF, simply reach out to your nearest Winners Chapel around you or call +2347080638000 or send a mail to contactcentre@lfcww.org, stating your location and requesting for any nearest WSF around you. You can also visit our Location page to search you location directory.faithtabernacle.org.ng

I want to attend Believer's Foundation Class

To attend BFC (Believers' Fellowship Class), you have two options. You can join online by visiting bfc.lfcww.org, where you can access teachings and resources. Alternatively, you can physically go to the nearest Winners Chapel in your area, where BFC sessions are held.
